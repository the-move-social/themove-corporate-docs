# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

---

**Date:** _________, 2026

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between:

- **Altin Lecaj** ("Assignor 1")
- **Eric Garcia** ("Assignor 2")
- **The Move Social, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Company" or "Assignee")

Each of Assignor 1 and Assignor 2 may be referred to individually as an "Assignor" and collectively as the "Assignors."

**Principal Office:** 10 River Rd Apt 10C, New York, NY 10044

---

## Recitals

A. The Company is a Delaware C-Corporation. The Move Social, Inc. ("TMS") is a Delaware corporation whose Class A Common Stock is held directly by the Assignors.

B. The Assignors are the founders of the Company and have been developing technology for "The Move" — an IRL social/adventure application — including the Reputation Engine, Vector Matching system, Experience Generation system, Safety Layer, and related infrastructure (collectively, the "Technology").

C. The Assignors desire to assign to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to all pre-existing intellectual property relating to the Company's business, and the Company desires to accept such assignment, on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

D. The Company intends to license certain intellectual property to TMS pursuant to a separate Intellectual Property License Agreement.

---

## 1. Assignment

### 1.1 Grant of Assignment

Each Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to all intellectual property conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by such Assignor, individually or jointly with the other Assignor, prior to the date of this Agreement, that relates to the Company's current or planned business (the "Assigned IP").

### 1.2 Scope

The Assigned IP includes, without limitation, all:

(a) Patents, patent applications, and patent rights;

(b) Copyrights and copyrightable works;

(c) Trade secrets and confidential information;

(d) Trademarks, service marks, trade names, and domain names;

(e) Source code, object code, and software;

(f) Algorithms, data models, and architectures;

(g) Designs, specifications, and documentation;

(h) All registrations, applications, renewals, and extensions of the foregoing; and

(i) All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement of any of the foregoing.

### 1.3 Worldwide and Irrevocable

The assignment made under this Section 1 is irrevocable and worldwide in scope. The Assignors shall have no further right, title, or interest in or to the Assigned IP following the execution of this Agreement.

---

## 2. Prior Inventions

### 2.1 Excluded Inventions

Each Assignor shall list on **Exhibit A** any prior inventions, original works, developments, improvements, or intellectual property that such Assignor wishes to exclude from the assignment made under Section 1 (the "Excluded IP").

### 2.2 Default

If an Assignor does not list any Excluded IP on Exhibit A, the assignment under Section 1 shall cover all intellectual property of such Assignor that relates to the Company's current or planned business.

### 2.3 Limitation on Exclusions

No item of Excluded IP may relate to the Company's current or planned business. If any item listed on Exhibit A is found to relate to the Company's business, it shall be deemed included in the Assigned IP notwithstanding its listing on Exhibit A.

---

## 3. Representations and Warranties

Each Assignor represents and warrants to the Company that:

### 3.1 Authority

Such Assignor has full right, power, and authority to make the assignment set forth in this Agreement and to grant the rights granted herein.

### 3.2 No Encumbrances

No Assigned IP is subject to any lien, encumbrance, security interest, or third-party claim.

### 3.3 No Prior Employer Resources

No Assigned IP was developed using the resources, facilities, or equipment of a prior employer or under any obligation or agreement with a third party that would conflict with the assignment made herein.

### 3.4 No Prior Assignment or License

Such Assignor has not previously assigned, licensed, transferred, or otherwise encumbered any of the Assigned IP to or in favor of any third party.

---

## 4. Further Assurances

### 4.1 Cooperation

Each Assignor agrees to execute any additional documents, instruments, or agreements and to take any further actions reasonably requested by the Company to evidence, perfect, register, or enforce the assignment and the Company's rights in the Assigned IP, including but not limited to filing patent, copyright, and trademark applications and assignments.

### 4.2 Power of Attorney

Each Assignor hereby irrevocably appoints the Company and its officers as such Assignor's attorney-in-fact, with full power of substitution, to execute and file any documents and to take any actions on such Assignor's behalf that may be necessary to perfect the assignment or otherwise carry out the purposes of this Agreement, in the event that such Assignor is unavailable or unable to act.

---

## 5. Consideration

### 5.1 Stock Purchase Opportunity

The assignment made under this Agreement is made in consideration of the stock purchase opportunity afforded to each Assignor under the Stock Purchase Agreement executed concurrently herewith, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which each Assignor hereby acknowledges.

### 5.2 Adequacy

Each Assignor acknowledges that the consideration described in Section 5.1 is adequate consideration for the assignment of the Assigned IP.

---

## 6. Future IP

The parties acknowledge that intellectual property conceived, created, or developed by either Assignor during the course of service to the Company following the date of this Agreement is governed by the Founders' Agreement (Section 10) and is not the subject of this Agreement. This Agreement covers only pre-existing intellectual property created prior to the date hereof.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

The terms and conditions of this Agreement are confidential and shall be subject to the confidentiality provisions of the Founders' Agreement. Neither party shall disclose the terms of this Agreement to any third party except as required by law or with the prior written consent of the other parties.

---

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

---

## 9. Miscellaneous

### 9.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the assignment of pre-existing intellectual property and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations, whether written or oral, relating to such subject matter.

### 9.2 Amendment

This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by all parties hereto.

### 9.3 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### 9.4 Counterparts

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
