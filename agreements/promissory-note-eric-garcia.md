# Promissory Note

**Principal Amount:** $10,000.00

**Date of Issuance:** March __, 2026

---

FOR VALUE RECEIVED, **The Move Social, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), hereby promises to pay to the order of **Eric Garcia** (the "Lender"), the principal sum of Ten Thousand Dollars ($10,000.00), together with accrued interest thereon, subject to the terms set forth below.

---

## 1. Interest Rate

This Note shall bear simple interest at the short-term applicable federal rate (AFR) in effect as of the date of issuance, which is **3.59% per annum** (March 2026 AFR, Rev. Rul. 2026-06), compounded annually.

## 2. Repayment

(a) **Maturity.** The outstanding principal balance, together with all accrued and unpaid interest, shall be due and payable on the earlier of:

   (i) Twenty-four (24) months from the date of issuance;

   (ii) The closing of an equity financing in which the Company raises aggregate gross proceeds of at least $500,000 (a "Qualified Financing"); or

   (iii) A Change of Control of the Company (as defined in the Founders' Agreement dated March 20, 2026).

(b) **Voluntary Prepayment.** The Company may prepay this Note in whole or in part at any time without penalty or premium.

(c) **Demand Limitation.** Consistent with Section 12.7(c) of the Founders' Agreement, the Lender may not demand repayment during any period in which repayment would render the Company unable to meet its ordinary operating expenses for the following sixty (60) days.

## 3. Priority

This Note shall be repaid before any dividends or distributions to stockholders of the Company, consistent with Section 12.7(b) of the Founders' Agreement.

## 4. No Equity Conversion

This Note is a debt obligation only. It does not entitle the Lender to any additional equity, voting rights, or preferential treatment beyond the repayment terms set forth herein, consistent with Section 12.8(d) of the Founders' Agreement.

## 5. Use of Proceeds

The proceeds of this Note shall be used solely for the Company's initial operating expenses, including but not limited to: technology infrastructure, domain registrations, software subscriptions, legal and filing fees, and other costs incident to the formation and early operations of the Company.

## 6. Governing Law

This Note shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

## 7. Cross-Reference

This Note is the promissory note referenced in Section 12.8 of the Founders' Agreement of The Move Social, Inc., dated March 20, 2026, and is subject to the terms and conditions thereof.
