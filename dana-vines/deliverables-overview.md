# Dana Vines — Role Expectations & Deliverables

**Role:** VP of Sales
**Start:** May 2026
**Compensation:** Equity (per Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement)

---

## What We Need From This Role

You own everything between the product and the market. That means getting The Move Social into the hands of consumers and getting local businesses signed up and actively using the platform. You are the bridge between what we build and who uses it.

---

## Three Core Responsibilities

### 1. Consumer Growth
Drive user acquisition in NYC through organic social media and influencer/creator partnerships. Build and run the content engine (TikTok, Instagram) and develop a network of NYC-based creators who authentically represent the brand. The goal is not just downloads — it's getting people to actually go on moves with their friends and come back.

### 2. Venue/Merchant Acquisition
Get local NYC businesses onboarded as venue partners. Pitch the value prop (free to join, pay-per-visit model), walk them through the merchant dashboard, and make sure they understand how to create moves, track performance, and see the value of being on the platform.

### 3. Go-to-Market Strategy & Execution
Own the overall GTM plan for NYC launch and the months that follow. This includes channel strategy, creator partnerships, venue sales process, and building repeatable playbooks that can scale to other cities down the line.

---

---

## How We Work Together

- **Weekly check-ins** with founders on key metrics: downloads, retention, venue partners, content performance
- **Monthly reviews** with a written summary of what worked, what didn't, and what's changing
- **Data-driven decisions** — no scaling spend until retention proves the product loop works
- **Autonomy on execution** — you decide how to hit these targets, we align on what the targets are

---

## Guiding Principles

- **Retention over vanity metrics.** 1,000 users who come back beats 10,000 who don't.
- **Real content, not brand content.** Raw, authentic, NYC-native. Not polished, not corporate.
- **Frictionless venue pitch.** Free to join, pay only on results, no contracts. Keep it simple.
- **Build the playbook.** Everything you do in NYC should be documentable and repeatable for the next city.
