# Dana Vines — Deliverables & Expectations

**Role:** VP of Sales (Independent Contractor)
**Start:** May 2026 (aligned with MVP launch)
**Focus Areas:** GTM execution, consumer acquisition (social + influencer), venue/merchant onboarding

---

## Month 1: May — Launch Activation & Foundation

### Consumer Acquisition
- Launch social media accounts (TikTok + Instagram) with 4-5 short-form videos/week
- Identify, brief, and activate 15-20 NYC micro-influencers (5K-150K following) for gifted seeding — product content drops staggered across launch week
- Execute waitlist activation: SMS blast to all signups on launch day
- Secure launch coverage through Gen Z-native channels: TikTok creator partnerships, Twitter/X accounts with NYC cultural reach, niche subreddits, and relevant newsletters/podcasts that skew 18-30
- **Target: 2,000 app downloads in first 7 days**
- **Target: 10+ creator partners actively posting**

### Venue/Merchant Onboarding
- Build and own the initial venue outreach list (first 10-15 targets in launch neighborhoods)
- Personally onboard 5-10 venue partners — walk them through the merchant dashboard and how location-based check-in/checkout works
- Deliver the "30-second pitch" to every venue: free to join, $1 per verified visit, no contract
- **Target: 10 active venue partners by end of May**

### Operations
- Establish weekly reporting cadence: downloads, D1/D7 retention, venue check-ins, content performance
- Own early user feedback collection — structured surveys from first cohort of users
- Monitor D1 retention daily; flag to product team immediately if below 40%

---

## Month 2: June — Validate & Expand

### Consumer Acquisition
- Analyze which content formats/creators drove the most installs — double down, cut what's not working
- Formalize partnerships with top 5 performing creators from launch seeding
- Begin testing referral mechanic promotion (in-app invite flow visibility, social sharing prompts)
- Soft-test paid social ($500-1,000 on Meta/TikTok) **only if D7 retention is above 35%** — kill if CPI exceeds $4
- **Target: 4,000-6,000 MAU**

### Venue/Merchant Onboarding
- Expand venue outreach to 25+ targets using May foot traffic data as proof
- Create a one-page "venue results" sheet showing real check-in data from Month 1 partners
- Begin training venue partners on the merchant dashboard for tracking move performance
- **Target: 15-25 active venue partners**

### Operations
- Deliver Month 1 post-mortem: what worked, what didn't, recommended pivots
- Weekly check-in with product team on outing completion rates and AI plan quality feedback

---

## Month 3: July — Scale What Works

### Consumer Acquisition
- Hit consistent posting cadence: 5 videos/week, POV outing content, before/after formats
- Launch "NYC discovery" content series — real moves, real venues, real people
- Expand creator network to 25+ active partners with clear performance expectations
- Scale paid social if retention benchmarks hold — defined budget TBD with founders
- **Target: 6,000 MAU**

### Venue/Merchant Onboarding
- Push toward 30-50 active venue partners across 3-5 NYC neighborhoods
- Lead every new venue conversation with hard foot traffic data from existing partners
- Identify which venue categories (bars, restaurants, coffee shops, activities) convert best — focus outreach accordingly
- Begin collecting venue testimonials for use in sales materials and press
- **Target: 30-50 active venue partners, $2,000-3,000/month venue revenue**

### Operations
- Present quarter-end review: user growth trajectory, venue revenue, retention health, channel ROI
- Recommend Month 4-6 strategy adjustments based on data

---

## Months 4-6: August-October — Growth Mode

### Consumer Acquisition
- Own the organic + creator acquisition engine end-to-end
- Build repeatable creator partnership playbook (outreach template, briefing, compensation structure, performance tracking)
- Launch "city seasons" content tied to NYC fall events and neighborhoods
- Target NPS score of 45+ — if below, diagnose and address
- **Target: 15,000 MAU by Month 6**

### Venue/Merchant Onboarding
- Scale to 75-100 active venue partners
- Build a scalable venue sales process: outreach templates, objection handling, onboarding checklist
- Ensure venues are actively using the merchant dashboard to track move performance and adjust their offerings
- Support product team on venue partner dashboard feedback and POS integration prioritization
- **Target: 75-100 active venue partners, $10,000-15,000/month venue revenue**

### Strategic
- Document the full GTM playbook (social, creators, venues, PR) as a repeatable system for future city expansion
- Begin identifying potential LA/Chicago venue contacts and creator networks for Year 2 planning
- Build press relationships with tech outlets (The Verge, Wired) for the privacy narrative angle

## Hard Rules

1. **No paid acquisition until D7 retention is confirmed above 35%**
2. **Content is real, not produced** — raw NYC footage, not brand-speak
3. **Venue pitch stays frictionless** — free to join, pay only on results, no contracts
4. **Weekly reporting is non-negotiable** — data drives every decision
5. **Quality over vanity metrics** — retention matters more than downloads
