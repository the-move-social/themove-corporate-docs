## Resolution 8 — Restricted Stock Grant — Dana Vines

**WHEREAS**, the Board has determined that Dana Vines will serve as VP of Sales and will be a key member of the founding team responsible for go-to-market strategy and sales, contributing full-time services in exchange for equity-only compensation with no cash salary;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has determined that a grant of 400,000 shares (4% of the Company's fully diluted capitalization) appropriately reflects the value of Dana Vines' anticipated contributions and the significant compensation risk she is assuming by forgoing cash compensation;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has determined that a restricted stock purchase at par value is the appropriate instrument for this grant, as it does not require a 409A valuation and allows Dana Vines to become a shareholder immediately upon commencement of services;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the following restricted stock award under the Equity Incentive Plan is hereby approved:

| Purchaser | Role | Shares | Purchase Price | Vesting | Relationship |
|-----------|------|--------|---------------|---------|-------------|
| Dana Vines | VP of Sales | 400,000 Class B | $0.00001/share ($4.00 total) | 4yr / 1yr cliff | Independent Contractor |

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the vesting commencement date for Dana Vines shall be the date on which she commences services to the Corporation, to be specified in the Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement shall include (a) a Company repurchase right on unvested shares at original purchase price, (b) immediate forfeiture of all shares upon termination for Cause, and (c) a Section 83(b) election backstop package.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to negotiate and execute an Independent Contractor Agreement with Dana Vines in connection with this grant.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that this restricted stock award does not require completion of a 409A valuation, as the Shares are being sold at par value and no stock options are being granted.

## Resolution 9 — Pool Management Covenant

**RESOLVED**, that the Board adopts the following pool management covenant:

(a) The restricted stock award authorized in Resolution 8 totals 400,000 of 1,500,000 shares reserved under the Plan (26.7% of the pool);

(b) 500,000 shares shall be reserved from the remaining 1,100,000 shares to satisfy any pool expansion requirement in connection with the Corporation's first institutional financing round;

(c) Before or concurrent with any seed or Series A financing, the Board shall seek an amendment to the Certificate of Incorporation to increase the authorized Class B Common Stock as needed to maintain a competitive option pool;

(d) The Class A stockholders shall provide the stockholder consent required for any such amendment.
