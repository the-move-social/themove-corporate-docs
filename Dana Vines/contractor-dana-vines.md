# Independent Contractor Agreement

## The Move Social Inc.

---

**Effective Date:** _________________

This Independent Contractor Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between:

- **The Move Social Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Company")
- **Dana Vines**, an individual (the "Contractor")

## 1. Engagement

The Company engages the Contractor, and the Contractor accepts such engagement, to perform the services described in this Agreement as an independent contractor.

## 2. Services

The Contractor shall provide sales leadership and business development services for the Company, including but not limited to:

(a) Sales strategy development and execution;

(b) Partnership development, client acquisition, and revenue growth;

(c) Market analysis and competitive positioning;

(d) Sales team building and contractor management as the Company grows;

(e) Such other sales and business development duties as agreed upon by the Board of Directors.

The Contractor shall determine the method, details, and means of performing the services. The Company may provide direction regarding the results to be achieved but shall not control the manner or means by which the Contractor achieves those results.

---

## 3. Compensation

### 3.1 Equity Compensation

The Contractor's sole compensation for services under this Agreement is a restricted stock award under the Company's Equity Incentive Plan, as set forth in a separate Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement executed concurrently with this Agreement.

### 3.2 No Cash Compensation

The Contractor acknowledges that no cash compensation, salary, wages, or benefits are provided under this Agreement. The Contractor is not entitled to any bonus, commission, health insurance, retirement benefits, paid time off, or any other benefit provided to employees.

### 3.3 Expenses

The Company shall not reimburse the Contractor for any expenses unless specifically approved in writing in advance by the Company.

---

## 4. Independent Contractor Status

### 4.1 Not an Employee

The Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. The Contractor shall not be entitled to any employment benefits, including but not limited to workers' compensation, unemployment insurance, health insurance, or retirement benefits.

### 4.2 Taxes

The Contractor is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes arising from compensation received under this Agreement, including self-employment taxes. The Company shall not withhold any taxes from payments to the Contractor. The Company will issue an IRS Form 1099 to the Contractor as required by law.

### 4.3 Control

The Contractor retains full control over the time, place, and manner of performing the services, subject only to the requirement that the Contractor meet agreed-upon deadlines and deliverables. The Contractor may provide services to other clients during the term of this Agreement, subject to the confidentiality obligations in Section 5.

### 4.4 Equipment

The Contractor shall provide all equipment, tools, and materials necessary to perform the services at the Contractor's own expense.

---

## 5. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property

### 5.1 CIIA Incorporation by Reference

The Contractor has executed, or shall execute concurrently with this Agreement, the Company's Confidential Information and Inventions Assignment Agreement (the "CIIA"), which is incorporated herein by reference. The CIIA governs all matters relating to confidentiality, intellectual property assignment, non-solicitation, and return of materials.

### 5.2 Conflict

To the extent any provision of this Section 5 conflicts with the CIIA, the CIIA shall govern.

### 5.3 Condition Precedent

Execution of the CIIA is a condition precedent to this Agreement taking effect. This Agreement shall not be binding until the CIIA has been fully executed by both parties.

---

## 6. Representations and Warranties

### 6.1 Contractor Representations

The Contractor represents and warrants that:

(a) The Contractor has the right and ability to enter into this Agreement and perform the services;

(b) The services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner;

(c) The Contractor's performance of services will not violate any agreement with any third party;

(d) The Contractor will not incorporate any third-party proprietary materials into deliverables without the Company's prior written consent.

### 6.2 No Conflicts

The Contractor represents that providing services under this Agreement does not and will not conflict with any other agreement, obligation, or duty of the Contractor.

---

## 7. Term and Termination

### 7.1 Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated by either party.

### 7.2 Termination Without Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason or no reason, upon **thirty (30) days** written notice to the other party.

### 7.3 Termination for Cause

"Cause" means (a) conviction of, or plea of guilty or no contest to, a felony or any crime involving fraud, dishonesty, or moral turpitude; (b) willful misconduct or gross negligence in the performance of duties; (c) material breach of this Agreement, the CIIA, or any other agreement with the Company that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice; or (d) willful failure to perform duties after written notice and a reasonable cure period.

The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Cause exists.

### 7.4 Effect on Equity

Upon termination of this Agreement, the Contractor's restricted stock shall be governed by the terms of the Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement and the Equity Incentive Plan, including without limitation the vesting, repurchase, and forfeiture provisions therein.

### 7.5 Survival

The CIIA (incorporated by reference in Section 5), Section 6 (Representations), and Section 9 (Dispute Resolution) shall survive termination of this Agreement.

---

## 8. Indemnification

The Contractor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from:

(a) The Contractor's breach of this Agreement;

(b) Any claim that the Contractor's services or deliverables infringe a third party's intellectual property rights;

(c) Any claim arising from the Contractor's failure to pay taxes or comply with applicable laws.

---

## 9. Dispute Resolution

### 9.1 Mediation

Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall first be submitted to mandatory mediation. The parties shall select a mutually agreeable mediator within ten (10) days of either party's written request for mediation. The mediation shall be completed within thirty (30) days of the mediator's appointment.

### 9.2 Binding Arbitration

If mediation is unsuccessful, the dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association in accordance with its Commercial Arbitration Rules, conducted in the State of Delaware by a single arbitrator. The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding and may be entered as a judgment in any court of competent jurisdiction.

### 9.3 Arbitration Costs

The Company shall bear all arbitration fees, arbitrator compensation, and administrative costs that exceed the amount the Contractor would have paid to file an equivalent action in state court in the State of Delaware. Each party shall bear its own attorneys' fees unless the arbitrator determines that one party's claims or defenses were frivolous, in which case the arbitrator may award reasonable attorneys' fees to the prevailing party.

---

## 10. General Provisions

### 10.1 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

### 10.2 Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the Confidential Information and Inventions Assignment Agreement, the Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement, and the Equity Incentive Plan, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

### 10.3 Amendment

This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement signed by both parties.

### 10.4 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

### 10.5 Assignment

The Contractor may not assign this Agreement without the Company's prior written consent.
